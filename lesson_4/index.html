Animal list

Penguin

A flightless bird with a distinctive black and white plumage, known for its playful waddling and excellent swimming abilities.

Elephant

Majestic land mammal characterized by its large ears, long trunk, and impressive tusks, symbolizing strength and intelligence.

Butterfly

Delicate insect with vibrant, iridescent wings, undergoing a remarkable metamorphosis from a caterpillar to a graceful flyer.

Giraffe

Tall, long-necked herbivore with distinctive spotted coat patterns, native to African savannas, and known for its towering presence.
